About the site
Ranobe-Honyaku is a fan translation community that facilitates the hosting of light novels being translated.

It's done differently to the only other community based platform (that I know of), Baka-Tsuki as it's not done in the style of a wiki. Founded in 2016 by Recchan after thinking MediaWiki was outdated. The site is built upon bulma.css and Python's micro-framework Flask using RethinkDB as the database.

Just to be clear; this isn't a translation group. Translators/Translation groups choose to come here and independantly translate and upload their translations; we have no input on what is translated or in what manner they translate, nor do we have control over how fast updates occur.
